[PROBLEM / AI-DRIFT]

General AI blends too many cognitive operations into one response.

Discovery, collaboration, orientation, imagination, and escalation are different kinds of work. When they collapse into one stream, complex environments become noisy.

Uncontrolled State

Normal AI tries to do everything at once.

It explains, brainstorms, diagnoses, predicts, summarizes, and recommends in the same motion. That can help in ordinary conversation and drift in high-load work.

Controlled State

Version 11.2 separates the operations.

The operator chooses the cognitive state. The system performs the kind of thinking required now, then stops where expertise begins.
